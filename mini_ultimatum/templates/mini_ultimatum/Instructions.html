Instructions

Three players are randomly selected. Your identity will also remain hidden from your the rest.

The three participants will have three different roles: the proposer, the responder and the punisher. You will be assigned randomly to a role, and it will be displayed on the next page.

The three of you will together receive {{ Constants.endowment }}. The experiment is about how to divide this amount. The proposer will make the responder a take-it-or-leave-it offer, which the responder can accept or reject. The punisher will have the option to penalize proposer based on his decision. If the offer is rejected, both proposer and responder receive {{ Constants.payoff_if_rejected }}, while the Punisher retains {{ Constants.endowment }}.

Punisher's role

The punisher does decide to penalize proposer based on his decision.

Results

In the end, when all players have made their decision, a results page will be displayed. Here the offer will be displayed once more, and whether it was accepted or rejected.

Furthermore, one of the following two payoffs will be displayed:

  • If the offer is accepted, the players will receive a payoff accordingly
  • If the offer is rejected, all players will receive {{ Constants.payoff_if_rejected }}
  • If the offer is accpeted, and the Punisher penalizes the Proposer then the amount penalized is deducted for both the Punisher and the Proposer.
