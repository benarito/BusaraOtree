Instructions

Three players are randomly selected. Your identity will also remain hidden from your the rest.

The three participants will have three different roles: the proposer, the responder and the punisher. You will be assigned randomly to a role, and it will be displayed on the next page.

The proposer and responder will together receive {{ Constants.endowment }}. The experiment is about how to divide this amount. The proposer will make the responder a take-it-or-leave-it offer, which the responder can accept or reject. The punisher will have the option to penalize proposer based on his decision. If the offer is rejected, both proposer and responder receive {{ Constants.payoff_if_rejected }}, while the Punisher retains {{ Constants.endowment }}.

Responder's role

While the proposer makes the offer, the responder can select which {{ Constants.offer_choices_count }} offers stated above to accept or reject. After the proposer has made the offer, it will be either accepted or rejected according to the responder's choices.

Punisher's role

The punisher does decide to penalize proposer based on his decision.

Results

In the end, when all players have made their decision, a results page will be displayed. Here the offer will be displayed once more, and whether it was accepted or rejected.

Furthermore, one of the following two payoffs will be displayed:

  • If the offer is accepted, the players will receive a payoff accordingly
  • If the offer is rejected, proposer and responder will receive {{ Constants.payoff_if_rejected }}, while the Punisher retains {{ Constants.endowment }}.
  • If the offer is accepted, and the punisher penalizes the proposer then the amount penalized is deducted for both the punisher and the proposer.
